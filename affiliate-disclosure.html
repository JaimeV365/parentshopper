Affiliate Disclosure

Transparency about how we make money and why you can trust our recommendations

How We Make Money

ParentShopper is a free resource for parents, but we do earn money through affiliate partnerships. Here's exactly how it works:

Affiliate Commissions

  • We earn small commissions when you buy products through our links
  • This typically ranges from 3-8% of the purchase price
  • You pay exactly the same price whether you use our links or not
  • We never mark up prices or charge you extra

This affiliate income helps us continue researching products, maintaining this website, and providing free advice to parents. Without it, we couldn't offer this service.

Our Commitment to Honesty

We want to be completely transparent about our affiliate relationships and how they affect our recommendations:

✓ We Only Recommend Products We Believe In

We turn down many affiliate opportunities because we don't believe in the products. Our recommendations are based on research and testing, not commission potential.

✓ We Tell You About Both Pros and Cons

Every product has limitations. We're honest about what works and what doesn't, even for products we recommend.

✓ We Don't Accept Payment for Positive Reviews

We never accept money from manufacturers in exchange for positive reviews. Our opinions are our own.

✓ We Update Our Recommendations

If we discover issues with products we've recommended, we update our reviews and remove recommendations if necessary.

Our Affiliate Partners

We work with several affiliate networks and retailers, including:

  • Amazon Associates: Our primary affiliate partner for most product recommendations
  • Other Retailers: We may include links to other retailers when they offer better prices or availability

We choose Amazon as our primary partner because of their exceptional customer experience and policies that benefit parents:

  • Smooth customer experience: Amazon's streamlined checkout process and reliable delivery make shopping stress-free for busy parents
  • No-questions-asked return policy: Most products can be returned within 30 days, which is crucial when you're not sure if a baby product will work for your family
  • Comprehensive product selection: Amazon carries virtually every baby product we recommend, making it a one-stop shop
  • Competitive pricing: Amazon's pricing is typically competitive, and they often have the best deals
  • Reliable shipping: Fast, reliable delivery is essential when you need baby products quickly

What This Means for You

Our affiliate relationships don't change your experience as a reader:

Your Price Stays the Same

You pay exactly the same price whether you use our links or go directly to the retailer.

You Get the Same Products

You receive the exact same products with the same warranties and return policies.

You Can Still Shop Elsewhere

We provide product names and details so you can find them anywhere you prefer to shop.

You Support Our Work

When you use our links, you help us continue providing free, honest product advice.

Our Editorial Independence

We maintain complete editorial independence in our product reviews and recommendations:

  • We choose which products to review based on parent needs, not affiliate opportunities
  • We test products ourselves or rely on extensive research and real parent feedback
  • We write honest reviews that highlight both strengths and weaknesses
  • We update our recommendations when new information becomes available
  • We refuse to recommend products we don't believe in, regardless of commission potential

Legal Disclaimer

This disclosure is provided in accordance with UK advertising standards and best practices for affiliate marketing. We are committed to maintaining the highest standards of transparency and honesty in all our content.
