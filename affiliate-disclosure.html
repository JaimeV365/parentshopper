Affiliate Disclosure

Transparency about how we make money and our commitment to honest reviews

How We Make Money

ParentShopper is supported through affiliate marketing. This means we earn small commissions when you purchase products through links on our website.

Important: This doesn't cost you anything extra. The price you pay is exactly the same whether you click our link or go directly to the retailer.

Our Affiliate Partners

We primarily work with Amazon Associates, as they offer the widest selection of products and competitive prices. When you see links to Amazon products, they are affiliate links.

We may also work with other retailers when they offer better prices or exclusive products that we believe would benefit our readers.

Our Commitment to Honesty

Our affiliate relationships never influence our recommendations. Here's how we maintain our independence:

  • We recommend products we genuinely believe in - regardless of commission rates
  • We're honest about product limitations - even when it might hurt sales
  • We don't accept payment for positive reviews - our opinions are genuinely ours
  • We update our recommendations - when better products become available
  • We disclose our affiliate relationships - transparency is important to us

Why This Matters

Affiliate marketing allows us to:

  • Keep our content free - no paywalls or subscription fees
  • Invest in research - buying products to test and review
  • Maintain our independence - we're not beholden to advertisers
  • Update our recommendations - as new products and information become available

Your Trust is Our Priority

We understand that trust is earned, not given. That's why we:

  • Clearly mark affiliate links when possible
  • Provide honest, balanced reviews that include both pros and cons
  • Update our content regularly to reflect current information
  • Respond to reader feedback and questions
  • Maintain strict editorial independence from our affiliate partners

Last Updated

This disclosure was last updated on October 18, 2025. We review and update this information regularly to ensure it remains accurate and complete.
