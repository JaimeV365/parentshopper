About ParentShopper

Your personal shopping guide for parenting - because every parent deserves honest, helpful advice

Our Story

ParentShopper was born from a simple frustration: as parents ourselves, we were tired of wading through endless product reviews that felt more like marketing than honest advice. We needed a place where we could trust the recommendations and know they came from real parents who had actually used the products.

After spending countless hours researching baby products for our own children, we realized we weren't alone. Every parent faces the same overwhelming decisions: Which pushchair will actually fit in our small car? What car seat is truly the safest? Which feeding products actually work?

So we created ParentShopper - a place where busy parents can find honest recommendations without the marketing fluff.

Our Mission

We believe every parent deserves access to honest, helpful product advice that respects their time and budget. Our mission is to help busy parents make smart purchasing decisions through:

  • Real parent experience: We share what we've learned from our own successes and mistakes
  • Honest assessments: We tell you what works AND what doesn't - no sugar-coating
  • Parent community insights: We listen to what other parents are saying about products
  • Budget-conscious advice: We understand that parenting is expensive, so we help you find value
  • Time-saving guidance: Get to the point quickly - we know you're busy

How We Share Information

We're just parents trying to help other parents avoid the mistakes we made and share the wins we discovered:

1. Real Parent Experience

We share what we've learned from actually using products with our own children - the good, the bad, and the "why didn't we know this sooner?" moments.

2. Parent Community

We listen to what other parents are saying - in online communities, forums, and real conversations. Parents are the best source of honest product feedback.

3. Safety First

We always check that products meet current safety standards and look out for any recalls or safety concerns that parents should know about.

4. Honest Assessment

We give you the real story - what works, what doesn't, and what we wish we'd known before buying.

Our Values

Honesty

We tell you the truth about products, even when it's not what you want to hear. No fake reviews, no hidden agendas.

Transparency

We're clear about our affiliate relationships and how we make money. Your trust is more important than any commission.

Respect

We respect your time, your budget, and your intelligence. No condescending advice or unnecessary complexity.

Practicality

We focus on what actually works in real life, not what looks good in marketing photos.

Who We Are

ParentShopper is run by parents, for parents. We're not a big company with fancy research teams - we're just parents who've been through the same struggles you're facing.

We've made our share of expensive mistakes, found some products that actually work, and learned a lot along the way. We share what we've learned to help other parents make better decisions and avoid the pitfalls we encountered.

We're not pediatricians or safety experts, but we do our best to stay informed about safety standards and recalls. When in doubt, we always recommend consulting with your healthcare provider.

Our Commitment to Standards

We're committed to maintaining the highest standards in everything we do:

Accessibility

This website meets WCAG 2.2 Level AA accessibility standards. We believe every parent should be able to access our recommendations, regardless of their abilities or the technology they use.

Privacy-First

We don't collect personal data, use tracking cookies, or store information about our visitors. Your privacy is protected - we focus on helping you, not tracking you.

Honest Reviews

Every recommendation comes with honest pros and cons. We tell you what works, what doesn't, and what to avoid. No fake reviews, no hidden agendas.

Transparency

We clearly disclose our affiliate relationships and explain how we earn commissions. You always know when we might benefit from a recommendation.

Our Affiliate Relationships

We want to be completely transparent about how we make money:

  • We earn small commissions when you buy products through our links
  • This doesn't affect the price you pay - you pay the same whether you use our links or not
  • We only recommend products we genuinely believe in, regardless of commission rates
  • We turn down many affiliate opportunities because we don't believe in the products
  • Our recommendations are based on our experience and what we've learned from other parents

This affiliate income helps us continue sharing our experiences and maintaining this site. Without it, we couldn't provide this service for free.
