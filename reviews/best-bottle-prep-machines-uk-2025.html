Are bottle preparation machines worth the money?

For families making 6+ bottles daily, absolutely yes. Our Perfect Prep saved 60-90 minutes daily compared to kettle method. Over six months, that's 180-270 hours saved. Do Perfect Prep machines really follow NHS guidelines?

Perfect Prep uses a modified approach: filtered tap water with initial hot shot above 70¬∞C to kill bacteria, then cooler water for drinkable temperature. Yes, all machines work with any UK formula brand including Aldi, Lidl, Tesco, and Sainsbury's own brands. We used several different brands in our Perfect Prep without issues. Baby Brezza requires setting adjustments for different formula densities, but works with virtually all brands.

How often do filters need changing?

Can machines break leaving me unable to make bottles?

Yes, mechanical failures happen occasionally. Keep kettle and manual bottle-making supplies as backup. Our Perfect Prep worked flawlessly for 3+ years, but we've heard reports of units failing after 6-18 months. Tommee Tippee's UK warranty and customer service generally handle replacements well.
