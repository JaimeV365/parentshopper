Frequently Asked Questions

Do I really need a bottle prep machine?

Not necessarily, but they save significant time. If you're spending 10-15 minutes per bottle with traditional methods, a prep machine reduces this to 2-3 minutes and ensures consistent temperature every time.

Save money by buying second-hand for 40-60% of retail price. These machines last years and are easy to clean thoroughly.

What's the difference between Classic and Day & Night versions?

The Classic version has basic operation with manual controls. The Day & Night version adds a digital display showing exact temperature and a night light for easier night feeds.

Both versions use the same proven technology and deliver the same reliable results. Choose based on whether you want the extra convenience features.

How important is consistent temperature really?

Extremely important for your baby's comfort and your peace of mind. Inconsistent temperatures can cause feeding problems and make your baby fussy.

If you primarily use traditional methods, temperature consistency becomes a daily challenge. If you plan to use a prep machine frequently, consistent temperature prevents constant frustration and ensures your baby is comfortable.

Can I use a bottle prep machine for different bottle sizes?

Yes, the Perfect Prep works with most standard bottle sizes. The machine adjusts automatically to different bottle heights and volumes.

Look specifically for machines that work with your preferred bottle brand and size. This compatibility affects enough parents that it deserves consideration before purchase.

Should I buy second-hand bottle prep machines?

Absolutely yes, with proper cleaning. These machines typically see 1-2 years maximum use before children outgrow them, meaning "second-hand" often means "barely used."

Check the machine for proper operation, ensure all parts are present, and clean thoroughly with hot water and soap. Avoid machines with damaged parts or signs of heavy use, as repairs often cost more than buying another second-hand alternative. We exclusively bought second-hand after our first expensive machine, saving hundreds of pounds across multiple purchases.
