Usage Guidelines

Amber Orange (#F59E0B)
Primary CTAs, "Smart Buy" indicators, important highlights. The action color that drives decisions and conversions.
Emerald Green (#059669)
"Recommended" badges, success states, quality indicators. Builds confidence and trust in recommendations.
Professional Typography
Outfit for logos and headings creates modern appeal, Inter for body text ensures accessibility and readability.
Accessibility First
All color combinations exceed WCAG AA standards (4.5:1 contrast ratio) ensuring busy parents can read easily on any device.
