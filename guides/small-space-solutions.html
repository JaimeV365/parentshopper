Space-saving checklist

Measure door frames

Compare collapsed pushchair width to your narrowest doorway. Aim for ≤ 52 cm.

Look for multi-use

Changing tables that become shelving or cots that turn into toddler beds cut storage needs in half.

Choose vertical storage

Add behind-the-door organisers for nappies, wipes and muslins instead of extra drawers.

Prioritise light frames

Lightweight gear (under 10 kg) is easier to lift into cupboards or up flight stairs.

What to do next

1

Audit your measurements

Note lift dimensions, hallway width and cupboard height before buying.

2

Prioritise daily-use items

Invest in the gear you touch every day first (pushchair, high chair, storage).

3

Delay bulky purchases

Borrow travel cots or bouncers short-term to test how they fit your space.
