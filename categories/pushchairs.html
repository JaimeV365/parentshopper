What We Look For

⚖️

Weight

6-7kg is the sweet spot for daily use. Lighter is better for travel, but don't sacrifice build quality.

📦

Folded Size

Compact fold is essential for small car boots and public transport. Umbrella fold is most practical.

🛒

Storage

XL storage basket is crucial for daily use. Check if it's included or sold separately.

🛡️

Build Quality

Frame should feel solid, wheels should roll smoothly, and harness should be easy to use.
