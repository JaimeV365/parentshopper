What to Avoid

❌ Heavy Travel Systems

Traditional travel systems weighing 15kg+ are impractical for daily use. Modern parents need lightweight, compact options.

❌ Cheap Umbrella Strollers

Budget umbrella strollers at entry-level pricing often have poor build quality, uncomfortable seats, and unreliable folding mechanisms.

❌ Overcomplicated Features

Avoid pushchairs with too many gadgets and features. Simple, reliable designs work best for daily use.
