What to Avoid

❌ Ultra-cheap options under £50

These often have poor build quality, uncomfortable seats, and may not meet safety standards. You'll end up replacing them quickly.

❌ Pushchairs without proper suspension

Essential for comfort, especially on uneven surfaces. Without it, your child will feel every bump and you'll struggle to push smoothly.

❌ One-size-fits-all claims

Every family's needs are different. Consider your car size, living space, and lifestyle before buying.
